Project Story

I collaborated with my team(Tech Dive Group 6) from the Hack.Diversity Fellowship to develop a web application that enables doctors to Create, Read, Update, and Delete (CRUD) structured radiology reports as part of their assessment of x-ray images for patients with COVID-19. The application provides an intuitive interface for doctors to input their observations and findings based on the x-ray images of COVID-19 patients. These structured reports generated by the application offer a clear and concise evaluation of the patient's condition, resulting in more efficient diagnosis and treatment.
